template<typename T, typename... Args>
struct bit::core::is_implicitly_constructible< T, Args >

Type trait to determine if T is implicitly constructible with braced initialization.

Note
Implicit construction is braced-initialization construction without specifying the type being constructed. This means that narrowing may occur if integral types are not matched correctly

The result is aliased as ::value
